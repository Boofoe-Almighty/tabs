==== Notes ====

An amazing track from III with obvious psychedelic/prog influences. It reminds me a lot of Colour
Haze, Motorpsycho, and Elder (the last two being on the same European label, in fact) -- lots of
complex chords and great orchestration.

Guitar I is panned left and Guitar II is panned right, both pretty hard.

I've listed the chords in certain partss for two reasons...

1) Given the layering of the two guitars (and possible double-tracking), it's a bit difficult to
hear the exact notes that are being played in all of the arpeggios. Ergo, while I feel that the
transcription is very close, I can't be 100% certain -- even having paid close attention to live
video footage [1]. As long as you play some variant of the chord for each arpeggio, however, it's
going to sound good.

2) Weedpecker seem like the kind of band that rarely play anything the same way twice. In the
spirt of improvisation, I would encourage you to try various different chord voicings and
inversions. Experiment and have fun!

[1] https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N4OFwA2aeS8
