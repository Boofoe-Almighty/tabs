==== Notes ====

The first track from Elder's debut album; a fairly straightforward stoner/doom affair with tons of
great riffs. \m/

Guitar I is the track on the left and Guitar II is on the right.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8V0ZvfE504
