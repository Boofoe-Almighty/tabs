==== Notes ====

If Riddle of Steel pt. 1 is Elder's homage to Sleep, then maybe pt. 2 is an homage to Fu Manchu?
Well... clearly it borrows from many different 70s-groove-based stoner metal bands. \m/

Guitar I is the track on the left and Guitar II is on the right.

I'm not aware of any live footage that exists of this track.
