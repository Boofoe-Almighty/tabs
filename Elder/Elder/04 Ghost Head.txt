==== Notes ====

This is a fairly straightforward stoner/doom track from the self-titled 2008 album, with no
shortage of amazing, heavy riffs.

I'm not exactly sure what Nick was using to record this album, unfortunately. It could very well be
the same guitar, amp, and pedals as used on DRS... which is to say, a Gibson '61 Reissue SG, a
modded Sound City 120, and, most prominently, a Black Arts Toneworks Pharaoh (fuzz). I'm not aware
of any evidence to support this, however. (Did the Pharaoh even exist in 2008? I *think* so?)

There's clearly a wah in a few parts as well, which he uses to varying effect. Sometimes it's very
obvious (solos), and other times it's used more like a phaser -- with a slow manipulation of the
expression across its range.

In short, who knows? It's not a difficult tone to achieve, however. A loud amp and a Muff-based
fuzz will get you most of the way there. It doesn't sound a million miles away from something like
an FZ-2 in boost mode either.
